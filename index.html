Who Is This Space For?

🔍 Explorers

Self-studying DBT, exploring it for the first time, or coming from a program with another provider? This is a place to practice alongside people who understand the work — and to discover whether a structured program like TheraHive is the right next step for you.

🐝 Active Students

Currently in a TheraHive cohort or another DBT program? Use this space to practice skills between sessions, get support when the homework feels hard, and stay connected to people who are doing the same work.

🎓 Graduates

Completed a DBT program and want to keep the skills alive? Stay connected to a community that takes the work seriously, get support when old patterns resurface, and build on everything you've already learned.

🩺 Practitioners

A therapist or counsellor who works with DBT clients? Deepen your own practice alongside the people you support, stay connected to the lived experience of doing this work, and contribute your expertise to a community that takes DBT seriously.

☝️ Why pay for community when there are free DBT groups on Facebook and Reddit?

Free communities are open to everyone, which means commitment levels vary widely. Our small monthly fee (less than a cup of coffee) does two things: it ensures that everyone here is genuinely invested in the work, and it allows us to keep investing in a community experience that's worth showing up for.
